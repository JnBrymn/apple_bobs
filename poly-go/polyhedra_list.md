# Complete List of Polyhedra

## 1. Platonic Solids (5)
Regular convex polyhedra with identical faces:
- Tetrahedron (4 triangular faces)
- Cube/Hexahedron (6 square faces)
- Octahedron (8 triangular faces)
- Dodecahedron (12 pentagonal faces)
- Icosahedron (20 triangular faces)

## 2. Archimedean Solids (13)
Convex polyhedra with regular faces of 2+ types, identical vertices:
- Truncated Tetrahedron
- Cuboctahedron
- Truncated Cube
- Truncated Octahedron
- Rhombicuboctahedron
- Truncated Cuboctahedron
- Snub Cube
- Icosidodecahedron
- Truncated Dodecahedron
- Truncated Icosahedron
- Rhombicosidodecahedron
- Truncated Icosidodecahedron
- Snub Dodecahedron

## 3. Kepler-Poinsot Polyhedra (4)
Regular star (non-convex) polyhedra:
- Small Stellated Dodecahedron
- Great Stellated Dodecahedron
- Great Dodecahedron
- Great Icosahedron

## 5. Uniform Polyhedra (75 non-prismatic)
Includes both convex and star polyhedra with regular faces:
- All 5 Platonic solids
- All 13 Archimedean solids
- All 4 Kepler-Poinsot polyhedra
- Plus 53 additional uniform star polyhedra
