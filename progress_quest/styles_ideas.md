# Progress Quest Style Ideas

## 1. **Retro Terminal (Current Original)**
- Dark background (#1a1a1a)
- Green text (#0f0)
- Monospace font (Courier New)
- Matrix/terminal vibe

## 2. **Colorful Modern (Current)**
- Purple gradient background
- Glassmorphism effects
- Fredoka One font
- Gold gradient numbers

## 3. **Neon Cyberpunk**
- Black background
- Neon pink/cyan borders
- Glowing text effects
- Dark mode with bright accents
- Grid background pattern

## 4. **Space Theme**
- Deep space background (dark blue/black with stars)
- Milky way gradient
- Futuristic font
- Floating planets in background
- Space ship or rocket icons

## 5. **Ocean/Underwater**
- Blue gradient (light to dark blue)
- Wave animations
- Bubbles floating
- Ocean-themed fonts
- Fish or sea creatures

## 6. **Retro Arcade**
- Pixelated fonts
- 8-bit colors
- Scanlines effect
- Retro game font
- CRT monitor look

## 7. **Nature/Forest**
- Green gradient background
- Leaves falling animation
- Tree/plant decorations
- Earth tones (brown, green, tan)

## 8. **Fire/Lava**
- Red/orange/yellow gradient
- Flame animations
- Dark background with fire effects
- Hot glowing numbers

## 9. **Ice/Winter**
- White/light blue gradient
- Snowflake animations
- Frosted glass effect
- Cool blue/cyan colors

## 10. **Candy/Sweet**
- Pastel colors (pink, yellow, light blue)
- Rounded, soft edges
- Bubble gum or candy decorations
- Playful, fun fonts

## 11. **Steampunk**
- Brown/copper colors
- Gear decorations
- Victorian-era fonts
- Rustic, mechanical look
- Metal textures

## 12. **Rainbow Party**
- Bright rainbow colors
- Confetti animations
- Fun, bouncy fonts
- Celebration vibe
- Colorful gradients

## 13. **Minimalist Clean**
- White background
- Simple black text
- Clean lines
- Lots of space
- Modern, professional look

## 14. **Glitch/Matrix**
- Digital glitch effects
- Matrix-style falling code
- Tech/cyber aesthetics
- Digital noise

## 15. **Galaxy/Cosmic**
- Deep purple/black with stars
- Nebula clouds
- Sparkle effects
- Mysterious, magical feel

## 16. **Sunset/Sunrise**
- Orange/pink/yellow gradient
- Warm, cozy feeling
- Soft glow effects
- Beautiful sky colors

## 17. **Ninja/Samurai**
- Dark background
- Red/black theme
- Japanese-style fonts
- Zen garden elements
- Minimal, powerful

## 18. **Wizard/Magic**
- Purple/blue mystical colors
- Stars and moons
- Sparkle effects
- Fantasy fonts
- Magical, enchanted feel

## 19. **Racing/Speed**
- Black/yellow checkered patterns
- Speed lines
- Racing font
- Fast, dynamic feel

## 20. **Food/Pizza**
- Red/white/cheese colors
- Pizza slices or food icons
- Fun, playful design
- Appetizing colors

## 21. **Vaporwave/Aesthetic**
- Pink/cyan/magenta
- Palm trees silhouette

## 22. **Dragon/Fantasy**
